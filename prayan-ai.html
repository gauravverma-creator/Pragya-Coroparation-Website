🔒 Privacy & Data Policy

Your Data is Safe

  • ✅ No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or track your trip planning data
  • ✅ Your API Key: Stored locally in your browser only (localStorage), never sent to our servers
  • ✅ Direct Communication: Your questions go directly to Google's Gemini API, we don't see them
  • ✅ No Account Required: Use Prayan AI completely anonymously
  • ✅ No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use analytics or tracking cookies

How It Works

Prayan AI runs entirely in your browser. When you enter your Gemini API key, it's saved locally on your device. Your trip questions are sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API. We never see or store your data.

Google Gemini API

This tool uses Google's Gemini API for AI-powered trip planning. Your data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing Google's terms before using this tool.
